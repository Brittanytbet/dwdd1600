The Basics of Fashion

The Color Theory
How different colors work together, or against each other, to affect our emotions and perceptions.
Balance
The visual weight in design.
Proportion
The interrelationship between parts of a design that make it aesthetically pleasing to the eye.
Rhythm
The repetition of visual patterns.
Harmony
The overall design of the garment or clothing item that creates visual unity.
Emphasis
A dominant point in an outfit that is created through the use of lines, shapes, textures, patterns, or colors.
