Welcome

Welcome to Fashion foundation! Here you will learn how to dress to look your best. You will learn about the newest and hottest fashion trends, how to dress for your body type, what outfits match what season, and what today's celebrities are wearing.

Style it

Creating outfits that match and look aesthetically pleasing can be hard. That’s why the Fashion Foundation is here to help! You’ll learn about the basics of:

  • The Color Theory,
  • Balance
  • Proportion
  • Rhythm
  • Harmony

Dress For Your Body Type

Struggle to dress for your specific body type? It's okay, together we can learn how to create aesthetically pleasing outfits that are sure to leave you feeling confident and comfortable in your body.
