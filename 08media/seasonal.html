Style It

Welcome

Depending on where you live, you'll experience different weather that can contribute to what you wear. If it's super hot outside, you're drawn to wear shorts. But if it's super cold outside, you'll want to wear layers. But the colors that are popular during different seasons are universal.

The Colors of the Season

Winter
In winter there will be a lot of cool tones. Some examples of cool tones are blues, purple, gray, and black.
Spring
For spring, people want to wear something light and airy. There will be a lot of pastel colors, such as pinks, light blues, greens, and cream colors.
Summer
In summer, you'll notice a lot of warm tone colors. These warm tone colors consist of reds, pinks, yellow, orange, and gold.
Fall
In fall, it starts to get colder, so the colors start to look more comforting. These colors consist of a lot of neutral and basic tones. These are brown, cream, black, and orange.
